About the Online Voting System

Our goal is to provide a simple, secure, and transparent way for communities to run elections online.

Purpose

This system enables administrators to register candidates and voters, while eligible voters can securely cast one vote per election. It is designed for student bodies, clubs, associations, and small organizations.

Transparency and Integrity

  • One-person-one-vote via email verification and unique voter records.
  • Immutable vote logs with timestamps for auditability (no ballot content exposure).
  • Real-time aggregate results without revealing individual choices.
  • Role-based access control separates admin tasks from voting actions.

How It Works

  1. Admins add candidates.
  2. Admins register voters or share a self-registration link.
  3. Voters verify their email and cast a vote.
  4. The system tallies results and prevents duplicate ballots.

Technology and Reliability

Built with a modern web stack and secured with HTTPS, input validation, and database constraints to ensure data integrity and uptime during voting windows.
