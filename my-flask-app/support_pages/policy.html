Privacy & Security Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-11

Scope

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data in the Online Voting System. It applies to voters, administrators, and observers who use the platform.

Data We Collect

  • Voter data: name, email, verification status, and voting eligibility.
  • Ballots: a cryptographic record that a ballot was cast; the specific choice is stored separately and never linked back to identity in reports.
  • Operational data: device/browser metadata, IP address, timestamps, and audit logs.

How We Use Data

  • To verify eligibility and enforce one-person-one-vote.
  • To tally results and prevent fraud or duplicate voting.
  • To secure the platform, detect abuse, and generate aggregated, anonymized statistics.

Security Controls

  • Transport security: all traffic is protected with HTTPS/TLS 1.2+.
  • Data at rest: sensitive data encrypted using AES-256 or a comparable standard.
  • Access control: role-based access (admin vs voter), least-privilege, and MFA for admins.
  • Application security: input validation, CSRF protection, rate limiting, and brute-force defenses.
  • Auditability: immutable logs for admin actions and ballot events with timestamps.
  • Backups: encrypted backups with tested restoration procedures.

Ballot Secrecy

We separate voter identity from ballot selections during tallying and reporting. Aggregate counts are published; individual choices are never revealed to administrators or other voters.

Retention

  • Voter records and logs: retained for the election period and audit window defined by the organizer.
  • Ballot data: kept only as long as necessary to verify results and comply with audit or legal requirements.

Your Rights

  • Access and correction of your registered information.
  • Deletion after the election period where legally permissible.
  • Right to lodge complaints with the organizing body or relevant authority.

Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. If email delivery or hosting providers are used, they are bound by data processing agreements and security standards.
