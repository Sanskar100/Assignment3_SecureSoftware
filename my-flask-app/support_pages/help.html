Help & Support

Guides and troubleshooting for voters and administrators.

Voter Guide

  1. Open the voting page and enter the email you registered with.
  2. Choose your candidate and select Cast Vote.
  3. If prompted, complete email verification via the code sent to your inbox.
  4. Confirm submission. You will see a success message; your vote is recorded.
Tip: You can only vote once. If you made an error, contact your election admin.

Admin Guide

  1. Add candidates with accurate names/party details.
  2. Register voters or share the self-registration link.
  3. Open/close the election window as scheduled.
  4. Monitor results in the dashboard; export CSV for auditing if enabled.

Common Issues

  • No candidates listed: ensure candidates are created and the election is open.
  • Can’t vote: confirm your email is registered and not already used.
  • No verification email: check spam; ask IT to allow-list the sender domain.
  • Browser problems: try a private window or another supported browser.

Security Best Practices

  • Never share verification codes or admin credentials.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable MFA for admin accounts.
  • Always access via the official HTTPS link.
